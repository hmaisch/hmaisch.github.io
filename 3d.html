3D design
Cubic Transformation
This project required taking a planar surface and creating three solid forms that use space dynamically. After creating the three different forms, I combined the three to create a form that included both flat and curved surfaces, and also having the three forms engaging multiple directions.
Wire Shoe
This project started out with a drawing of a shoe, which was then used to create a wire structure of the shoe that included clear structure, volume and correct proportion. Along with this, the structure included varied line weight through the use of different wire gauges, and the use of negative space to emphasize the cross-contours.
