about me

hi there!

My name is Haley Maisch and I am currently studying Graphic Design at Kansas State University. I first became interested in the art of graphic design during my junior year of high school and since then, I have gained such an appreciation for all things design-related. I am the type of person who thinks and communicates better visually, and I feel that design and art in general can be used to communicate with others in ways that sometimes, words can't accomplish. On this site, you'll find some of the work I've completed here at KSU over the last couple of years, as well as links to my social media. I love learning about and creating with people, so free to reach out if you ever want to collaborate or even just need a friend to chat with!
